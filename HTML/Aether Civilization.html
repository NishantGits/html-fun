Governance

Democracy Reimagined

Government as service, not authority.

The Circle of Wisdom

127 citizens selected through sortition (random lottery) from each expertise district: Science, Ethics, Arts, Infrastructure, and Wellness. No campaigns, no donations. AI-facilitated deliberation ensures every voice is heard. Decisions require 80% consensus—forcing genuine collaboration.

Direct Democracy

Every citizen votes on major decisions via neural-interface. Average participation: 94%. Proposals can be submitted by any citizen with 1,000 signatures. AI ensures proposals are constitutional and scientifically sound before voting.

Justice System

No prisons. No police. AI mediation resolves disputes. Rehabilitation is restorative—harm done is repaired through community service and education. The few who cannot coexist are offered passage elsewhere, not punishment.
