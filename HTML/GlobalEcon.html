Conclusion

The data suggests that interdependence is not uniform. While the United States uses globalization to export high-value services, Brazil uses it to fund industrial expansion. However, for Ethiopia, the reliance on global markets often results in "importing" inflation and debt, making the need for diversified trade more urgent than ever.
